## Core Tagline

You make a wish. We make it real.

## Story

`phonowell` is a well for creation.
You can drop anything in: an image, a document, a URL, a fragment, a reference.
Then you make one wish.
The well resonates, reorganizes what matters, and lifts one complete artifact to the surface.

## Why Now

Chat-first collaboration with AI is too expensive.
People spend most of their time on two actions:

1. telling AI what they want
2. feeding AI the resources it needs

`phonowell` compresses this into an asset-first flow:
put ideas and resources in one place, then let AI analyze, connect, complete, and generate.

## Narrative Pillars

1. Everything can enter as an asset.
2. Nothing is lost: every asset has traceability.
3. One well, one wish, one final artifact.
4. AI is the orchestrator, not the owner of intent.
5. Fewer chats, stronger outputs.
